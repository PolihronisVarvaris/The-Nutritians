How we deliver

Week 1: Initial Consultation & Needs Analysis

Meeting with the restaurant team to understand their specific needs, dietary requirements, and how they want to present calorie information.

Week 2: Calorie Counting & Staff Training

Collecting menu data and calculating nutritional information for all dishes.
Providing training to restaurant staff on how to present and explain nutritional information to customers.

Week 3: Creating Promotional Materials

Design and creation of promotional materials to support the rollout of the calorie counting service,
tailored to the restaurant's brand and its audience.

Week 4: Service Launch & Promotion

Official launch of the service and promotion to customers, both online and offline.
Ongoing support and feedback.
